Bread

The evolution of bread throughout human history: what it symbolized and how it reflected the values of society.

Bread exists in every country around the world. From Indian naan to the French baguette and Venezuelan arepas, bread has been a staple food and fundamental to the structure of society since before humans even left their hunter-gatherer nomadic lifestyles and settled to establish agricultural societies. Bread has been a symbol of life, of culture, civilization, poverty and wealth, war and peace.
